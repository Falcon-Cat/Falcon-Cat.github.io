Tony Howard's Bio

Tony Howard is a junior at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science, Drama with a minor in Dance. He knew he wanted to make a career in computer science ever since taking AP Computer Science in highschool where he first learned Java. Growing up in Minnesota he liked to spend a lot of his time outside. He would bike, snowboard, and play frisbee whenever he could. He also played tennis until he chose to persue performing with the same intensity he put into sports. Now at college he sings and dances with Torn Ticket II, the musical theater club, which he is also on the board for as the media archivist.
